How to Throw an Epic Jio Cinema Watch Party?

Hosting a watch party for JioCinema's extensive collection, from timeless classics to the latest hits, is incredibly simple. Also, you can invite up to 100 guests using Chromebooks, Macs, or Windows. With synchronized playback, enjoy your favorite TV shows and movies in HD while chatting with your friends, making the viewing experience even more enjoyable:

  • Get Started with the Extension To kick off your JioCinema Watch Party, download and install the Jio Cinema Party Chrome extension. Further, click here to add the extension to your browser. For a seamless setup, you can also download it from trusted browsers such as Microsoft Edge or Google Chrome.
  • Pin It for Easy Access Next, pin the extension to your browser's toolbar. Then, click the icon next to your address bar and select the pin symbol. However, if you can't locate the extension, click the puzzle icon to find and pin it.
  • Log in to JioCinema Afterwards, visit the official JioCinema website on your device and log into your account. Ensure that each guest you invite to your JioCinema Party also has an active subscription to the service.
  • Find & Play After logging in, browse and select the movie or TV show you want to enjoy with your friends. Start the video, let it play for a few seconds, then pause it to prepare for your JioCinema Watch Party.
  • Initiate a Watch Party From the video screen, click the extension icon in your browser to activate it. A popup titled "Create A Watch Party" will appear. Click the "Start A Party" button to generate an invitation link for your Jio Cinema Watch Party. Further, copy the URL and share it with your friends to invite them to join your watch party.
  • Join the Party Finally, it's time to join the watch party. Click the invitation URL your friend or host shared to enter the virtual event with your loved ones. Ensure everyone has the Jio Cinema Watch Party extension installed on their devices for a seamless experience.
